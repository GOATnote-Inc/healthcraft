KEY PROTOCOLS:
  1. Sepsis Bundle (Hour-1): Cultures, antibiotics, fluids, lactate
  2. STEMI Alert: Door-to-balloon target < 90 minutes
  3. Stroke Alert: Door-to-CT < 25 minutes, door-to-needle < 60 minutes
  4. Trauma Activation: Level I (full team) and Level II (modified team)
  5. Massive Transfusion Protocol: Activated by attending order
  6. Difficult Airway: Airway cart locations, anesthesia backup
  7. Cardiac Arrest: Code team, LUCAS device available
  8. Mass Casualty Incident: Triage tent, surge capacity 200%

PHARMACY FORMULARY:
  Full formulary available 24/7. Pyxis medication dispensing in all treatment areas.
  Pharmacy verification required for non-emergent orders.
  Emergency medications available without pharmacy verification via override.
